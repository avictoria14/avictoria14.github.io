UNDERSTANDING GEN AI

• Gen AI creates entirely new content, such as stories, music, and images, rather than retrieving existing information like search engines.

• Tools like ChatGPT and Gemini demonstrate how GenAI can assist with creativity, problem-solving, and generating innovative ideas.

• Gen AI is designed to be accessible and versatile, benefiting both casual users and professionals.

FEATURES AND USAGE

• Gen AI tools allow users to interact using text, voice, or images, making them adaptable to different needs.

• Crafting effective prompts requires including context, specifying tasks, and defining desired output formats for better responses.

• Applications range from writing emails to generating creative ideas for personal or professional use, and so much more!

RISKS OF GEN AI

• Data privacy risks emerge when personal or sensitive information is shared with AI platforms.

• GenAI can generate misinformation, like fake news or deepfakes, which can mislead or harm individuals and communities.

• Copyright challenges exist, as determining ownership of AI-generated content is complex and could lead to legal disputes.

MANAGING THE RISKS

• Verify the reliability of sources, check for inconsistencies, and use AI detection tools to confirm content authenticity.

• Learn to identify deepfakes by observing unnatural visuals, mismatched audio-visual elements, or other irregularities.

• Maintain critical thinking and assess content from multiple perspectives to make informed decisions..

INTERACTIVE LEARNING

• Quizzes and activities focus on teaching users to create better prompts by including context, tasks, and formats.

• Practical exercises encourage hands-on learning with tools like ChatGPT and Gemini to build proficiency.

• Activities help users develop confidence in using GenAI effectively while promoting responsible usage practices.
